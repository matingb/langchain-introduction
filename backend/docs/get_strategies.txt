=== BROCK (Pewter City Gym Leader) ===
Type: Rock
Recommended Minimum Level: 12-14

Team:
- Geodude (Lv.12)
- Onix (Lv.14)

Strategy:
Rock-type Pokémon have high defense but low special defense. Water and Grass moves are extremely effective. Early-game players often struggle due to limited access to strong counters.

Recommended Approach:
- Use Water-type Pokémon like Squirtle or Grass-types like Bulbasaur.
- Special attacks are more effective than physical attacks against Brock’s team.
- Butterfree is a strong alternative due to Confusion (special damage).

Notes:
Avoid relying on Normal or Flying-type Pokémon, as they are ineffective against Rock-types.


=== MISTY (Cerulean City Gym Leader) ===
Type: Water
Recommended Minimum Level: 18-21

Team:
- Staryu (Lv.18)
- Starmie (Lv.21)

Strategy:
Water-type Pokémon are fast and can deal strong special damage. Starmie is particularly dangerous due to its speed and recovery moves.

Recommended Approach:
- Electric-type Pokémon like Pikachu are highly effective.
- Grass-types also perform well but must handle speed disadvantages.
- Focus on dealing damage quickly before Starmie can recover.

Notes:
Avoid Fire, Rock, and Ground Pokémon, as they are weak or ineffective.


=== LT. SURGE (Vermilion City Gym Leader) ===
Type: Electric
Recommended Minimum Level: 24-26

Team:
- Voltorb (Lv.21)
- Pikachu (Lv.18)
- Raichu (Lv.24)

Strategy:
Electric Pokémon are fast and can paralyze your team, disrupting strategy.

Recommended Approach:
- Ground-type Pokémon are immune to Electric attacks.
- Use Pokémon like Diglett or Dugtrio for strong counters.
- Prioritize speed to avoid paralysis chains.

Notes:
Avoid Water and Flying Pokémon.


=== ERIKA (Celadon City Gym Leader) ===
Type: Grass
Recommended Minimum Level: 29-32

Team:
- Victreebel (Lv.29)
- Tangela (Lv.24)
- Vileplume (Lv.29)

Strategy:
Grass Pokémon rely on status effects and gradual damage.

Recommended Approach:
- Fire, Flying, Bug, and Ice-type moves are effective.
- Use fast attackers to avoid status conditions.
- Fire-types like Charmeleon are excellent choices.

Notes:
Watch out for Sleep Powder and Poison effects.

=== SABRINA (Saffron City Gym Leader) ===
Type: Psychic
Recommended Minimum Level: 40-43

Team:
- Kadabra (Lv.38)
- Mr. Mime (Lv.37)
- Venomoth (Lv.38)
- Alakazam (Lv.43)

Strategy:
Psychic Pokémon deal high special damage and are very fast.

=== GIOVANNI (Viridian City Gym Leader) ===
Type: Ground
Recommended Minimum Level: 47-50

Team:
- Rhyhorn (Lv.45)
- Dugtrio (Lv.42)
- Nidoqueen (Lv.44)
- Nidoking (Lv.45)
- Rhydon (Lv.50)

Strategy:
Ground Pokémon deal strong physical damage and have good coverage.

Notes:
